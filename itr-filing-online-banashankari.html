FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does ITR filing cost in Banashankari? ▾
ITR filing in Banashankari typically costs ₹999 to ₹15,000 depending on your income type. Salaried returns are the most affordable, while business and capital gains returns cost more.
Can I file my ITR online with a Banashankari CA? ▾
Yes, all CAs provide fully online ITR filing. You can upload documents digitally and receive your ITR acknowledgement without visiting any office.
What documents are required for ITR filing? ▾
Common documents include PAN, Aadhaar, Form 16, bank statements, and investment proofs. Freelancers and businesses may need additional records.
What is the deadline for ITR filing? ▾
For individuals, the deadline is usually 31 July. For audit cases, it extends to 31 October. Late filing is allowed with penalties.
Which tax regime should I choose? ▾
It depends on your deductions and income. A CA will compare both regimes and suggest the most beneficial option.
