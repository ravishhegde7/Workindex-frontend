Tally vs Zoho Books — which is better for my Bangalore business? ▾
Tally is preferred by traditional businesses, CAs, and manufacturing units in Bangalore for its offline reliability and widespread CA familiarity. Zoho Books is better for cloud-first startups and e-commerce businesses needing real-time dashboards and integrations. Most Bangalore accountants on WorkIndex are proficient in both — they'll recommend based on your business type.
