How much does a basic business website cost in Mangalore? ▾
A basic 5-page business website in Mangalore typically costs ₹8,000–₹20,000. A WordPress site with CMS, contact form, and mobile responsive design runs ₹15,000–₹35,000. What payment structure should I use when hiring a Mangalore developer? ▾
WorkIndex recommends milestone-based payments for all development projects. A standard structure: 30% upfront to start work, 40% on completion of core features or design approval, and 30% on final delivery and handover. Avoid paying 100% upfront regardless of the developer's reputation or location.
