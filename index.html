Terms & Conditions

💳 Credits & Payments

  • Credits are purchased upfront and are non-refundable under any circumstance.
  • Credits are consumed when you unlock a client's contact details and send an approach.
  • Purchasing credits does not guarantee you will win the client — it gives you the opportunity to pitch.
  • WorkIndex is a lead generation platform, not a job guarantee platform.
  • Credit packages are one-time purchases with no expiry date.

📨 Approaches & Leads

  • Only 5 experts can send an approach on any single client request — first come, first served.
  • Responding early significantly increases your chances of being selected — clients tend to engage with the first few approaches they receive.
  • Credits spent to unlock a request are not returned even if the client is unresponsive, hires someone else, or cancels.
  • WorkIndex does not control or influence which expert a client chooses.
  • You are responsible for your own communication, pricing, and delivery after connecting with a client.

🔍 Direct Hire via Explore

  • Clients may discover your profile through the Explore section and invite you directly to their request.
  • Credits are still required to respond to a direct invite and unlock the client's contact details.
  • A direct invite signals client interest but does not waive the credit requirement.
  • Direct invites are based on your profile visibility, ratings, and profile strength score.

👤 Profile & Profile Strength

  • A complete profile significantly increases your chances of being discovered and invited by clients.
  • Profile strength is based on: photo, bio, specialization, services, location, experience, and ratings.
  • Experts with higher profile strength appear higher in search results and receive more direct invites.
  • You must provide accurate information about your qualifications and experience.
  • Misrepresenting credentials is grounds for immediate account suspension with no credit refund.

📁 Documents & File Access

  • Clients may share documents with you through the platform for the purpose of completing their requested service.
  • You may request document access only when it is genuinely required to assess or deliver the service.
  • After a client grants you access, you may download and use documents solely for delivering the requested service.
  • Downloaded documents must not be shared with third parties, used for unrelated purposes, or retained after the engagement ends.
  • Misuse of client documents will result in immediate permanent ban and may be reported to relevant authorities.
  • Once a client revokes access, you must immediately cease use of any information obtained from those documents.

⭐ Ratings & Conduct

  • Clients can rate you after work completion and those reviews are public.
  • WorkIndex does not remove negative reviews unless they violate platform guidelines.
  • You are expected to maintain professional conduct at all times.
  • WorkIndex reserves the right to remove profiles with consistent negative ratings or reports.

📋 Posting Requests

  • You can post service requests for free.
  • You are responsible for providing accurate and complete information in your request.
  • Posting fake or misleading requests may result in account suspension.
  • Each request is visible to experts matching your service category and location.

📨 How Expert Selection Works

  • A maximum of 5 experts can approach you per request — ensuring only serious, relevant proposals.
  • Experts who respond early are more committed and available — review approaches promptly for best results.
  • You are under no obligation to hire any expert who approaches you.
  • Once you select an expert, we recommend marking your request to avoid further approaches.

🔍 Exploring & Directly Hiring Experts

  • You may browse verified expert profiles in the Explore section and shortlist or invite experts directly.
  • Inviting an expert directly does not cost you anything.
  • You are responsible for verifying an expert's credentials and suitability before hiring.
  • WorkIndex profiles show ratings and reviews from past clients to help you decide.

📁 Documents & File Sharing

  • You may upload documents to your account and share them with experts you are working with.
  • Nothing is shared without your explicit approval — you control all access.
  • You may grant or revoke document access to any expert at any time.
  • WorkIndex stores your documents securely but is not liable for misuse after you have granted access.
  • Do not upload documents containing passwords, bank PINs, or unrelated sensitive personal data.
  • Report any misuse immediately through the support ticket system.

💰 Payments & Hiring

  • All payments for services are made directly between you and the expert.
  • WorkIndex does not handle, hold, or guarantee any payments between clients and experts.
  • Any disputes over payment, quality, or delivery must be resolved between you and the expert directly.
  • WorkIndex is not liable for the outcome, quality, or timeliness of any service delivered.

⭐ Reviews

  • You are encouraged to leave honest ratings after work is completed.
  • Reviews help other clients decide and help good experts grow.
  • False or malicious reviews intended to harm an expert may be removed and your account suspended.

📌 General (Applies to Everyone)

  • WorkIndex is a marketplace that connects clients with professionals — we are not a party to any service agreement between them.
  • WorkIndex reserves the right to update these terms at any time.
  • Continued use of the platform means you accept the current terms.
  • Accounts found to be fraudulent or abusive will be permanently banned.
  • Banned expert accounts forfeit any remaining credits with no refund.
  • For disputes or misuse reports, raise a support ticket — our team will review within 48 hours.
