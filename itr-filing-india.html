FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for ITR filing? ▾
For salaried individuals: Form 16 from employer, bank statements, investment proofs (80C, 80D), and AIS (Annual Information Statement) from Income Tax portal. Your CA will guide you on exactly what's needed.
What is the last date for ITR filing? ▾
For individuals (non-audit): 31 July of the assessment year. For businesses requiring audit: 31 October. Belated returns can be filed by 31 December with a late fee under Section 234F.
How long does ITR filing take? ▾
Simple salaried returns are typically completed within 24–48 hours once you share your documents. Business and complex returns may take 3–7 days depending on the volume of records.
Is it safe to share my documents online? ▾
WorkIndex has a secure document sharing system. You control access — only share with the CA you choose to work with. You can revoke access at any time.
Old tax regime vs new tax regime — which is better? ▾
It depends on your deductions and income level. A verified CA on WorkIndex will compare both regimes for you and recommend the one that minimises your tax liability.
