FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions — Web & App Development Bangalore

Are Bangalore developers more expensive than developers in other Indian cities? ▾
Generally yes — Bangalore developer rates are 15–30% higher than Tier 2 cities due to demand and living costs. However, Bangalore's talent pool is also deeper, especially for React, Flutter, Node.js, and SaaS products. WorkIndex lets you compare Bangalore developers with those from Mysore, Hubli, or remote Karnataka — helping you balance cost and quality for your project.
How should I structure payments with a Bangalore developer? ▾
Always use milestone-based payments — never pay 100% upfront. A common structure is 30% on project start, 40% on milestone completion (e.g., working prototype or core features), and 30% on final delivery and testing. WorkIndex strongly recommends this to all clients working with any developer, including those in Bangalore.
Flutter vs React Native — which should I choose for my Bangalore startup's app? ▾
Both are excellent cross-platform choices popular with Bangalore developers. Flutter has better performance and UI consistency across devices; React Native has a larger community and easier JavaScript-to-mobile transition for web developers. Bangalore has strong talent pools for both. The developers you receive quotes from on WorkIndex will recommend based on your specific app requirements.
Should I build an MVP first for my Bangalore startup product? ▾
Yes — especially in Bangalore's competitive startup ecosystem. An MVP saves 40–60% of initial budget, gets you to market faster, and validates the idea before full investment. Bangalore has many developers specialised in MVP development for early-stage startups. Post your requirement mentioning MVP to get relevant quotes.
Does the developer handle hosting and deployment for my Bangalore business? ▾
Most Bangalore developers will set up hosting and deployment as part of the project — AWS, GCP, Vercel, DigitalOcean, or shared hosting depending on your budget and scale. Recurring hosting costs (₹3,000–₹20,000/year depending on server) are paid by you directly. Clarify this in the scope discussion before the project starts.
