FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions — GST Services Bangalore

Do I need GST registration for my Bangalore startup? ▾
If your annual turnover exceeds ₹20 lakh (₹10 lakh for services in certain states), GST registration is mandatory. E-commerce sellers and those making inter-state supplies must register regardless of turnover. Many Bangalore startups register voluntarily even below the threshold for credibility and ITC benefits. A GST consultant can advise on your specific situation.
How much does monthly GST filing cost in Bangalore? ▾
Rates vary by business size. Small businesses in Bangalore with turnover under ₹20L/month typically pay ₹500–₹2,500 per month for GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B. Mid-size businesses pay ₹2,500–₹6,000. Post on WorkIndex to compare actual quotes from Bengaluru consultants.
What is ITC reconciliation and why does my Bangalore business need it? ▾
Input Tax Credit (ITC) reconciliation ensures the GST paid to your vendors matches what they've filed on the portal (GSTR-2A/2B). Mismatches can result in ITC denial and notices from the department. Bangalore businesses with many vendors — especially in IT, manufacturing, and trading — need this done regularly.
I received a GST notice for my Bangalore business — what should I do? ▾
Don't ignore it. GST notices have strict response timelines — missing them leads to ex-parte assessment orders and heavy penalties. Post your requirement on WorkIndex immediately and experienced Bangalore GST consultants will review the notice and guide your response. Most notice types can be resolved without personal visits to the department.
What are the GST return filing deadlines I need to follow? ▾
GSTR-1: 11th of following month (monthly filers). GSTR-3B: 20th of following month. QRMP filers: quarterly GSTR-1 by 13th of month after quarter. GSTR-9 (annual): 31 December. Late fees apply from the due date. Your GST consultant will set up reminders to keep you on track.
