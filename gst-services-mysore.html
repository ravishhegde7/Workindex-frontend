FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions — GST Services Mysore

I sell Mysore silk — what GST rate applies and do I need to register? ▾
Silk fabric attracts 5% GST. If your annual turnover exceeds ₹20 lakh, registration is mandatory. For inter-state sales, registration is required regardless of turnover. Mysore GST consultants on WorkIndex are familiar with the textile sector's specific GST provisions and can guide you on registration and compliance.
Does my Mysore hotel or resort need a separate GST registration? ▾
Yes. Hospitality businesses in Mysore need GST registration if turnover exceeds ₹20 lakh. Hotels with room tariff above ₹1,000 per day charge 12% GST. Restaurants within hotels may have different rates. GST consultants on WorkIndex experienced in Mysore's hospitality sector can handle your registration and monthly compliance.
Can a Mysore GST consultant handle my filing if I'm based in Mandya or Hassan? ▾
Yes. GST filing is entirely online, so your consultant's location doesn't matter. A Mysore-based GST consultant registered on WorkIndex can handle compliance for businesses in Mandya, Hassan, Chamrajnagar, Kodagu, and all surrounding districts without any physical visits required.
What is the QRMP scheme and is it right for my Mysore business? ▾
QRMP (Quarterly Return Monthly Payment) allows businesses with turnover up to ₹5 crore to file GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B quarterly instead of monthly, while making monthly tax payments. This reduces the compliance burden significantly. Most small to mid-size Mysore businesses qualify. A GST consultant will advise if it suits your business.
