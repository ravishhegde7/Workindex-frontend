FAQ

Photography — Common Questions

What should I ask a photographer before booking? ▾
Ask about: number of edited photos delivered, delivery timeline, raw files policy, album/print options, backup equipment, cancellation policy, and whether travel charges apply for your location.
How far in advance should I book a wedding photographer? ▾
Top wedding photographers in India are typically booked 6–12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (Oct–Feb). For other events, 2–4 weeks notice is usually sufficient.
Do photographers travel to other cities? ▾
Yes, many photographers on WorkIndex are open to outstation assignments. Travel and accommodation costs are typically charged separately. Mention your location in your requirement post so photographers can include travel costs in their quotes.
Photography only vs photography + videography? ▾
Adding videography typically increases cost by ₹15,000–₹50,000 for weddings. For events, a short highlight reel adds ₹5,000–₹15,000. Mention if you need video in your requirement — many photographers offer combined packages at a discount.
