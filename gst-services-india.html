FAQ

GST — Common Questions

Who needs GST registration? ▾
Any business with annual turnover above ₹20 lakh (₹10 lakh for North-East states) must register. E-commerce sellers, inter-state suppliers, and businesses making taxable supplies below the threshold can also register voluntarily.
What is the QRMP scheme? ▾
QRMP (Quarterly Return Monthly Payment) allows businesses with turnover up to ₹5 crore to file GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B quarterly while making monthly tax payments. A GST consultant on WorkIndex can advise if it suits your business.
What happens if I miss a GST filing deadline? ▾
Late fees apply — ₹50/day (₹20/day for nil returns), subject to a maximum cap. Interest at 18% per annum on outstanding tax liability also applies. A GST consultant can help you file overdue returns and minimise penalties.
How long does GST registration take? ▾
Typically 3–7 working days after submitting all documents. The consultant handles the GST portal application, document submission, and follows up with the department on your behalf.
