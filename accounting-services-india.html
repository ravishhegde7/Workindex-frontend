FAQ

Bookkeeping — Common Questions

What is included in monthly bookkeeping? ▾
Typically: recording all income and expenses, bank reconciliation, accounts payable/receivable tracking, monthly P&L statement, and balance sheet. Ask your accountant what's included in their quote.
Tally vs Zoho Books — which is better? ▾
Tally is preferred by traditional businesses and CAs for its offline reliability. Zoho Books is better for cloud-based teams who need real-time access. Your accountant on WorkIndex can advise based on your business type.
How do I share my records with the accountant? ▾
WorkIndex has a secure document sharing system. You can also share bank statements via email or give read-only access to your accounting software. Your accountant will guide you on the most efficient method.
Can an accountant also handle my GST filing? ▾
Yes — many accountants on WorkIndex offer bundled GST + bookkeeping packages which are often 20–30% cheaper than hiring separately. Mention GST in your requirement post to get bundled quotes.
